— About

Biography

Milica Ikonić Nešić, MSc, works as a teaching assistant at the Department of Library and Information Science, Faculty of Philology, University of Belgrade.

She completed her bachelor's and master's studies in mathematics, with a major in Theoretical Mathematics and Applications, at the Faculty of Mathematics, University of Belgrade. She is currently in the final stage of her PhD studies in Intelligent Systems at the Multidisciplinary Doctoral Studies, University of Belgrade.

Her research focuses on language technologies for the Serbian language, primarily semantic analysis of literary texts and the application of language models (LMs) to works of Serbian literature.
